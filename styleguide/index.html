Style Guide

Overview

Mighty Life Fitness (MEF) is a strength and conditioning gym for young and middle-aged adults looking to become stronger and more fit. It offers straightforward and affordable gym membership as well as training packages with highly-experienced coaches from various backgrounds.

Fitness is a lifestyle, not an activity. Mighty Life Fitness projects this mantra by having a brand concept that connects the idea of strength and power with the classical element of earth, which symbolizes fortitude and growth. MLF also aims to be approachable and inspiring to potential members, thus it harnesses lightweight and earth-like aesthetics to convey a positive vibe while deviating away from the typical “raw” black and white theme of strength gym brands.

Typography

Alfa Slab One
A slab typeface is used for its weight and its projection of solidity in relation to the brand.

Open Sans
A font that adapts well to screens with comfortable legibility.

  • h1. Heading 1

    Alfa Slab One / 600 / 2.37rem (38px)

  • h2. Heading 2

    Open Sans / 600 / 1.7777rem (28px)

  • h3. Heading 3

    Open Sans / 600 / 1.3333rem (21px)

  • p. Body text

    Open Sans / 300 / 1rem (16px)

  • small. Small text

    Open Sans / 300 / 0.75rem (12px)

Buttons

Button

Class: .btn
Use this for non-critical or secondary actions such as the reset button.

Code

Primary Button

Class: .btn .btn--primary
Use this for important actions such as the submit button.

Code

<input type="submit" class="btn btn--primary">

Grid

This site uses a flexible grid system. Simply a add div.row that contains div.col with .col--{number} to establish a row of column(s). For example, a div.col.col--3 creates a column that fills 1/3 of the row. There are up to 12 column sizes to choose from.
